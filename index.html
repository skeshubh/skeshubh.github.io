About me

I'm a results-oriented Software Engineer with a strong focus on robotics, automation, and scalable system architecture. My academic journey includes a Master’s in Robotics & Automation Engineering from Worcester Polytechnic Institute and a Bachelor’s in Computer Science & Engineering from RCOEM. I’ve cultivated a robust technical foundation through both education and industry experience, allowing me to design and implement fault-tolerant, high-performance systems across diverse applications.

Professionally, I’ve interned with Cognizant Technologies and Pranam Creative Solutions, where I worked on backend systems using Java EE, Spring Boot, and AWS services. My work involved developing secure, reliable software solutions and contributing to cloud-integrated IoT devices like autonomous medicine dispensers. I also led multiple robotics and automation projects, including a wearable healthcare monitoring system, a SLAM-based 3D reconstruction pipeline, and a dynamic path planning framework for non-holonomic robots in warehouse simulations.

Beyond technical skills, I take pride in my leadership and collaborative abilities. As President of the SRCOEM Robotics Club, I guided a 30-member team through hands-on workshops and successful participation in ROBOCON 2022. I’m passionate about solving real-world problems with intelligent systems, and I thrive in environments that encourage innovation, cross-disciplinary collaboration, and continuous learning.
